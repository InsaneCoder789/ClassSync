What ClassSync provides

ClassSync helps users organize academic workflows by combining Google Classroom read-only sync, optional Gmail-based reminder discovery, manual tasks, local reminders, planners, widgets, study planning, and exam preparation features.

04

Google-connected features

  • Google sign-in is required for Classroom and optional Gmail integrations.
  • Google Classroom access is read-only for supported app features.
  • Gmail sync is optional and should only be used for academic reminder discovery when enabled by the user.
  • Google availability, account permissions, Workspace policies, or API limits may affect app behavior.

User responsibilities

  • Use the app only with accounts and academic data you are permitted to access.
  • Review synced tasks, reminders, and planning suggestions before relying on them for deadlines or submissions.
  • Delete local exports from your device when they are no longer needed.

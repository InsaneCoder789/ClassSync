What it does

One surface for coursework, reminders, and planning.

  • Imports Google Classroom coursework, announcements, and materials into local academic flows.
  • Optionally reads Gmail academic reminders when the user enables Gmail sync.
  • Combines synced work with manual tasks, planners, reminders, and home screen widgets.
  • Uses on-device classification and rules to organize academic updates into usable task signals.

Availability

Android-first release, cross-platform roadmap later.

ClassSync is currently published as an Android application. The current public pages exist to support app distribution, tester onboarding, Play compliance, and Google OAuth verification. Cross-platform expansion may come later, but the current product and testing flow are Android-only.
